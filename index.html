FAQ

The questions a sharp founder will ask before replacing a homepage.

Why is “DECENTRALIZED KICKSTARTER” the right lead message?

It establishes the category in two seconds, then gives Angor the room to prove why its architecture creates a meaningfully better trust model than traditional crowdfunding.

Does this design over-index on aesthetics at the expense of clarity?

No. The visual drama exists to support comprehension: Bitcoin, milestones, recoverable funds, and Nostr are all surfaced through modular sections with explicit hierarchy.

Can this design scale as Angor adds more product and ecosystem content?

Yes. The layout is built from reusable content blocks, so new screens, ecosystem modules, testimonials, or technical explainers can slot in without collapsing the system.

Why make the protocol mechanics visible instead of hiding them in docs?

Because the mechanics are the differentiator. Hiding them weakens the pitch. Good design turns those mechanics into desire rather than homework.
